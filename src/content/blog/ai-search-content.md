AI answer engines prioritize clarity and structure.

If you want your content surfaced in AI-generated responses, focus on directness: concise definitions, clear headings, and evidence-backed claims.

## Formatting checklist

- Use one clear H1 and logical H2/H3 hierarchy.
- Answer core questions in the first 2-3 paragraphs.
- Include examples, stats, or original insight.
- Add internal links to related resources.
- Keep paragraphs short and readable.

The best AI-optimized content is still written for humans first.
